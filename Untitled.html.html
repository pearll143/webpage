Timeline of Space Exploration

Pre-Space Age

  • 1903: First Successful Powered Flight by Wright Brothers
  • 1926: First Liquid-Fueled Rocket Launched by Robert Goddard
  • 1935: First Photograph of Earth from the Stratosphere

Early Rocketry

  • 1942: First Suborbital Flight by V-2 Rocket
  • 1947: First Animals in Space
  • 1957: Sputnik 1 Launched by USSR

Space Race

  • 1961: Yuri Gagarin Orbits Earth
  • 1969: Apollo 11 Moon Landing
  • 1971: First Space Station (Salyut 1)

Modern Era

  • 1998: International Space Station (ISS) Assembly Begins
  • 2004: Mars Rovers (Spirit and Opportunity) Land on Mars
  • 2020: SpaceX Crew Dragon Demo-2
